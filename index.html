My short story...

I would define myself as an earthling, as activist for a new future, and as a political analyist if such thing exists

I was born and raised in Uruguay around 1991 CE. In many ways, I was fortunate enough to grow up in a relatively favorable context, which later gave me the opportunity to travel and realize that “society”, as it exists today, is marked by many injustices and untapped potential

This realization opened my eyes and ignited a strong commitment to understanding sociopolitical issues, which led me to university and afterwards. At this point, after many years, I have the luck to be dedicated to this area, and try to use whatever I discover to help build a better present and foster a prosperous future for generations to come—a mission I continue to pursue to this day
