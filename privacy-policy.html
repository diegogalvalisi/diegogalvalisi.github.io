Privacy policy

Protecting your data, as is our own

Last updated: January 2026

I Diego Galvalisi (together with my affiliates, "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects personal data when you visit the website, listen to the podcast, subscribe to the mailing list, or engage with any other interactive part.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable EU data protection laws.

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for the processing of personal data on this website is:

  • Website owner: Diego Galvalisi
  • Location: Italy
  • Contact email: diegogalvalisi@gmail.com

2. Personal Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following personal data:

a) Subscription Data
When you subscribe to our mailing list, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (only if you choose to provide it)

b) Website & Podcast Usage Data
When you visit the website or listen to the podcast, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address (anonymized where possible)
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and basic usage statistics

This information is collected for analytical and technical purposes.

3. Purpose of Data Processing

Your personal data is processed for the following purposes:

  • To send podcast updates, newsletters, and related communications (only if you subscribe)
  • To operate and improve the website and podcast
  • To analyze website traffic and performance
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR, the legal bases for processing personal data are:

  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR) – for email subscriptions
  • Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) – to maintain and improve the website and podcast
  • Legal obligation (Article 6(1)(c) GDPR) – where required by law

You may withdraw your consent at any time.

5. Email Subscriptions

If you subscribe to the mailing list:

  • You will receive emails related only to the project
  • You can unsubscribe at any time via the link included in every email
  • Emails are sent using a third-party email service provider that complies with GDPR

6. Cookies

This website may use cookies or similar technologies to:

  • Ensure proper website functionality
  • Collect anonymous analytics data

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

7. Data Sharing & Third Parties

Personal data may be processed by trusted third-party service providers, such as:

  • Email marketing platforms
  • Website analytics services
  • Hosting and streaming platforms

These providers process data on our behalf under GDPR-compliant data processing agreements.

8. Data Storage & Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Personal data is stored only for as long as necessary to fulfill its purpose or to meet legal requirements.

9. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority

To exercise your rights, please contact us at diegogalvalisi@gmail.com.

10. Children's Privacy

This website and podcast are not specifically directed at children under the age of 16.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
